Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By click on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random numer. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Every time the game restarts the value of each crystals will change.
